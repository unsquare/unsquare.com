About Me

Hi, I'm Jeff James! In my spare time, I am a writer.

I recently had the incredible opportunity to join the writer's room for Camp Camp season 4, an animated show about misfit campers produced by Rooster Teeth.

I wrote three episodes solo and co-wrote part of an anthology episode. My first episode, The Quarter-Moon Convergence, premiered on June 29th, 2019.

I am currently working on new short fiction as well as some original scripts of my own.

I write about books at Full of Words, and I previously wrote about video games at GamerSushi. I love nothing more than a good bit of critical analysis and intelligent discussion, and do my best to bring those skills to all my work.

Many years ago, I wrote episodes of both Web Zeroes and The Leet World for my friends at Smooth Few Films. I also wrote most of the as-yet unfinished third season of The Leet World.

In October of 2012, the now-defunct Kazka Press published my story Burning Love.

I do the occasional web development work, and I am the webmaster for both Smooth Few Films and GamerSushi. I have extensive experience with the WordPress platform, but decided to convert my personal website to run on Jekyll. Figuring out how to use a static site generator was definitely an interesting challenge.

If you'd like to get in touch, you can find me on Facebook, Twitter and Tumblr. My email address is available upon request if you have a burning need. To keep up with what I'm reading, look me up on Goodreads. I read a lot.
